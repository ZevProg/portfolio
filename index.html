Two ways to set your loan: (1) type your debit balance straight in, or (2) enter your current account equity and tap the button — the app sets loan = market value − equity. Either way, Net Equity then tracks live as prices move, and the interest rate below grows the loan a little each day until you update it. Note: the margin-shares column does NOT set the loan on its own — your loan is the fixed cash you borrowed, not the value of those shares. Leave this at 0 if you have no margin.
